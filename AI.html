Artificial Intelligence

It's become a new thing now to try and get computers to replicate human intelligence and cognitive ability, and grasp abstract concepts. That's the whole concept of AI.

Examples of use

Artificial Intelligence is everywhere, and you may not know it. Some examples can include:

  • Smart home systems. Things like Google Home and Amazon Alexa use voice recognition systems to understand what is being said to them and respond accordingly
  • Chatbots. Applications like ChatGPT have been on the rise recently, all because of their use of AI
  • Grammar checkers. Something as simple as a grammar-check application like Grammarly uses AI to check the spelling and grammar of a sentence
  • Self-driving vehicles. A car that can drive itself needs AI to know what is around it and when to stop

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Increase convenience. Having AI search through a database instead of searching saves you time
  • Could potentially save lives. Medicine is one of the fields that are taking AI under its wing. With the help of AI, we have the potential to fight off diseases quicker, saving lives

Cons

  • Could intrude on privacy. Things that use AI akin to Alexa and Google Home need to be on all the time so that they can hear what you say at any given moment, which could be a breach of privacy
  • What happens if it goes wrong? If something goes wrong, who's at fault, and what would we do against it?
