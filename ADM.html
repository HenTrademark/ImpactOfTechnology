Automated Decision making

Nowadays, it's become more and more popular to make algorithms that would make decisions for you. As these can drastically affect people's lively-hoods, their use in big decision-making has been greatly debated.

Examples of use

Theres a few examples of computers being at the forefront of decision-making:

  • Social media feeds. Algorithms have been used because they can show the content that you are most likely to click on using your past interactions or connections (through followers or contacts).
  • Stock trading. Algorithms can detect when a stock has fallen too far and automatically pull out, or even take past data to predict the future
  • Plant automation / Power distribution systems. They are used here as they need to respond to changes instantly

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Increase productivity. These being in place help alleviate work from employers for accepting people for example
  • Could potentially save lives Algorithmic decision can be faster than the human reaction time, allowing them to make decisions faster than we can. This could save lives in a driver-less car for example

Cons

  • Could treat people unfairly. Machines that use Automated Decision-Making could treat people worse than others because of its inability to understand context
  • Keeps people on websites. Some websites have gotten so good at making algorithms that people are stuck on their apps
